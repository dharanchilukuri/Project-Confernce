About the Conference


The IEEE International Conference on Breakthrough in Heuristics and Reciprocation of Advanced Technologies (BHARAT-22) will be held at GITAM Deemed to be University, Visakhapatnam from March 04 to 05, 2022. This conference is jointly organized by GITAM Deemed to be University in association with Vizag Bay section. The main aim of this conference is to provide a platform for the academicians, researchers, engineers, industrial participants and budding students around the world to share their research aspects and technological advances with the global experts and also to explore the latest trends in the cutting edge technologies.
